A Room of One's Own – a joint publication

A joint publication by me and six other students at HDK, inspired by the essay A Room of One's Own.

A Room of One's own is an essay written by Virgina Woolf. Inspired by the first chapter, I, like the character, documented a walk to different places I am familiar with. At these places which are located around central Gothenburg I photographed printed excerpts from the book. In the publication I also added a double-sided page which gives a hint as to where the photographs were taken.
