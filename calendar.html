Calendar

A calendar tool to schedule tasks.

The tasks are presented in the sidebar, while also being represented by a number within the calendar. By clicking a particular date in the calendar the tasks will be filtered to only show the tasks for that day. Tasks are saved to local storage, and can be both edited and removed. A group project by me, Felicia Segerholm and Isabell Betar.
